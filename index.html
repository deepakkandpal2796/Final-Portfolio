ABOUT

I'm a Front-End Developer from New-Delhi/India.
I am passionate about building excellent website that improves the lives of those around me. I specialize in creating website for clients ranging from individuals to businesses. I have serious passion for UI effects, animations and creating intuitive, dynamic user experiences.

SERVICES

01. Brief

To get a clear picture of what you need we start every project by receiving an initial brief of what you want. After understanding completely we start making overview.

02. Design

This is where we apply the paint to your website. we'll take the concepts we've agreed on discussion to make them look usable, modern and clean.

03. Develop

When the coding begins there will generally be less communication from our side as this is quite an involved process that doesn't require much feedback.
